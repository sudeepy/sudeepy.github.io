Over the past few years mobile gaming has been heralded as the death of traditional console/handheld-based gaming. The ease of purchasing, access to an ever-growing library of games, and the growing presence of mobile devices* have all been cited as reasons why mobile gaming has become an important part of the gaming industy*. However, one key downside to mobile gaming that has not been discussed is game abandonment.



The iOS App Store opened in 2008, almost 7 years ago. According to Wikipedia, there are over 1.3 million apps* on the App Store today.
